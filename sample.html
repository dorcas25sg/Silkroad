The Silk Road

A final project for MUSA 620: Data Wrangling and Data Visualization (Instructor: Max Galka)

By Dorcas Chang & Ziqin Wang


The ancient silk road is one of the most important trade routes that links many ancient civilizations from the East to West. These trade routes carried people, goods, knowledge, culture, and technology that had left their marks on the history and development of the civilizations they served (UNESCO, n.d). Our project intends to create an interactive map of the ancient Silk Road to educate and communicate a visual representation of this indelible segment of world history. Due to the sheer scale of the Silk Road, our project will focus specifically on the Chinese segment of the Silk Road for now, which contains over half (2,485 miles/4,000 kilometers) of the entire length of 4,350 miles/7,000 kilometers (Travel China Guide, 2017).
